4th Annual 5K for PPPD Awareness

Date: Saturday, July 11th, 2020

Location: Pinehill Park, Mason, OH

All the proceeds raised from our 5k will go to the Mayo Clinic for further research on this terrible condition. To date, we have raised almost $30,000 for the cause.

Divisions

  • 9 & under
  • 10-14
  • 15-19
  • 25-29
  • 30-34
  • 35-39
  • 40-44
  • 45-49
  • 50-54
  • 60-64
  • 65 & over

Awards

The top overall male and female runners will receive trophies. Medals will be given to the top male and female runners in each age division

After the Race

A variety of food and drink will be provided (Chipotle, Nothing Bundt Cakes, etc)

Registration Fee

Registration before the Race online is $25 and Registration on Race Day is $30.

3rd Annual 5K for PPPD Awareness

Date: Saturday, July 13th, 2019

The HERO Club hosted its 3rd annual 5k this year on July 13th. We raised over $11,000 and had over 150 people in attendance.

Congrats to our winners Anthony Goodrich and Isabella Rojo, who ran a 19:16 and 25:51 respectively.

We are very thankful for all our volunteers and sponsors:

  • Diamond Sponsors – UST Global
  • Platinum Sponsors – McDonald & McDonald Attornies
  • Food Sponsors – Fresh Market, Chipotle
  • Water Sponsors – Drug Mart

Once again, thanks to everyone who came. We wouldn’t have been able to do this without everyone’s support.

2nd Annual 5K for PPPD Awareness

Date: Saturday, July 24th, 2018

We are very thankful to everyone that came to the run. The event was a huge success. We had about 150 runners and walkers who participated in the race. Looking back, it’s very surreal that it’s already been almost a year since the first annual The HERO Club 5k and that we’ve already completed the second one. We are very grateful to all the support and contributions we got from the volunteers, sponsors, donors, and runners. So once again, thanks to each and everyone of you.

We also had dazzling singers Danielle Ullman, Mckenzie Stewart, Gabe De Guia, Priyanka Yenugu and Shelby Sansom from the MELODY WALLACE VOICE STUDIO, who entertained the crowd with their mesmerizing voice and songs.
